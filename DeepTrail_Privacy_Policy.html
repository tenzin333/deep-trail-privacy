DeepTrail — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

DeepTrail ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that helps users build a personal knowledge graph from their web browsing. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

The short version: DeepTrail processes web page content to build your personal knowledge graph. Your data is sent to our backend server for AI processing (embedding and summarization). We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising. You can delete your data at any time.

1. What Data We Collect

When you use DeepTrail, we collect the following data:

  • Web page content: Text content extracted from web pages you choose to save to your knowledge graph. This is used to generate embeddings and summaries.
  • Page metadata: The URL, page title, and timestamp of saved pages. This is used for node identification, deduplication, and display in the knowledge graph.
  • Authentication data: If you create an account, we store your email address and an authentication token to identify your account and secure your data.
  • Search queries: Queries you type into DeepTrail's search are sent to our backend to perform semantic search across your knowledge graph.

2. What Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your general browsing history. We only process pages you explicitly save.
  • We do not collect passwords, form data, financial information, or health information.
  • We do not track your activity across websites.
  • We do not collect data when the extension is inactive.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide DeepTrail's core functionality:

  • Embedding generation: Page content is sent to the Gemini API (Google) to generate semantic embeddings (numerical representations) that enable similarity search in your knowledge graph.
  • Summarization: Page content is sent to the Groq API to generate summaries displayed in your knowledge graph nodes.
  • Semantic search: Your search queries are embedded and compared against your stored knowledge graph to find relevant nodes.
  • Knowledge graph storage: Your nodes, edges, and embeddings are stored in our PostgreSQL database to persist your knowledge graph across sessions.

4. Third-Party Services

DeepTrail uses the following third-party services to process your data:

  • Google Gemini API — for generating text embeddings. Data sent: page content text. Gemini API Terms.
  • Groq API — for LLM-powered summarization and query responses. Data sent: page content, search queries. Groq Terms.
  • Neon.tech — for PostgreSQL database hosting. Data stored: nodes, embeddings, metadata, account info. Neon Privacy Policy.
  • Render — for backend API hosting. Data processed: all API requests transit through Render's infrastructure. Render Privacy Policy.

We do not sell or share your data with any other third parties. Data is sent to the services listed above solely to provide DeepTrail's functionality.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your knowledge graph data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Neon.tech.
  • All data transmission between the extension and our backend uses HTTPS encryption.
  • All data transmission between our backend and third-party APIs (Gemini, Groq) uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using Chrome's chrome.storage API.
  • Each user's data is isolated — you cannot access another user's knowledge graph.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Your data is retained as long as you have an active DeepTrail account.
  • You can delete individual nodes from your knowledge graph at any time through the extension UI.
  • You can request complete deletion of all your data by contacting us at the email address below. We will delete all your data within 30 days of receiving your request.
  • If you uninstall the extension, your data remains on our server until you request deletion. This is so you can reinstall and recover your knowledge graph.

7. Chrome Web Store Limited Use Compliance

DeepTrail's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We limit data use to providing and improving DeepTrail's single purpose: building a personal research knowledge graph.
  • We do not transfer user data to third parties except as described in Section 4, which is necessary to provide DeepTrail's core functionality.
  • We do not use or transfer user data for personalized advertisements.
  • We do not sell user data to data brokers or information resellers.
  • We do not use or transfer user data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — your entire knowledge graph is visible in the extension UI.
  • Delete your data — delete individual nodes via the UI, or request full account deletion.
  • Export your data — (planned feature) export your knowledge graph data.
  • Opt out — you can stop using DeepTrail and uninstall the extension at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated through the Chrome Web Store listing and/or within the extension. Continued use of DeepTrail after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
